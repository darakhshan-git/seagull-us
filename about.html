About Us

SEAGULL MARITIME
AGENCIES

Seagull been operating under the registered name of Seagull Maritime Agencies Private Limited, a company incorporated in Mumbai, India since 1992.

Seagull has since expanded globally with offices currently in India, UK, and USA. The company plans to extend its business with offices in Europe, South-East Asia and Africa soon.

We at Seagull are committed to being your logistics partner that offer efficient and customized logistic solutions for your business needs. We strive to provide unparalleled services to you, so your business grows globally. Apart from our cutting-edge infrastructure, we also have experienced and dedicated personnel coupled with state-of-the-art IT solutions. We are actively involved in Freight Forwarding, Customs Clearance, ICD Operations, Rail & Road Transportation, and other trade related expertise.

Vision

To become a one-window value based global logistics solutions provider.

To provide global connectivity and become a leading provider of personalized all-round logistical solutions with the highest level of customer satisfaction.
